About Us

Sehat Medical Complex(SMC) is a state of medical and healthcare network established under the umbrella of prestigious institute The University of Lahore in October 2021, aims to provide state-of-the-art diagnostic and treatment facilities. Located in the heart of Lahore and conveniently linked to all areas mostly through the Orange Line and Metro Bus Services, Sehat Medical Complex renowned itself for providing services par excellence at affordable rates.

At Sehat Medical Complex our qualified and renowned experts work to diagnose and cure diseases, utilizing advanced clinical skills, sophisticated diagnostic equipment, and treatment protocols to provide patients with the best possible treatment. Housing different diagnostic, curative and rehabilitative services under one roof, Sehat Medical Complex provides a holistic approach for all healthcare problems.

Mission Statement

To bring together world-class services and qualified specialists together to provide effective treatment facilities at affordable rates.

Vision Statement

SMC will emerge as a leading local healthcare facility, that shall advance the frontiers of clinical service delivery and research by developing an enabling environment of compassion, respect, teamwork, and innovation.
