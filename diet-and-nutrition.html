Diet and Nutrition

Let’s Build Wellness, rather than Treating Disease

Via SMC, we thoughtfully and gently guiding individuals on the unique paths toward Optimal Health, Wellness or improving a Medical Condition. We Assess, Diagnose and Manage Nutritional Problems. We provide you with Customized Meal Plans and Home-Based Workouts. All Programs are Customized according to Client’s Physical Conditions, Body Needs, Food Availabilities and Preferences. We deal with every individual separately with a Unique Approach for all that meets their Demands and Needs.

Our Complete Nutrition Care Process includes:

• Nutrition Assessment

•Nutrition Diagnosis

• Nutrition Intervention

• Nutrition Monitoring & Evaluation

We break all the myths about Conventional meaning of “Dieting” by having an influence with our amazing Dietary Management and Counseling Programs. We started this organization when the issue of obesity and related Health Concerns started to increase in our society alongside the fast and accelerating Hectic Lifestyles, we brought along the best line of Dietitians and Nutritionists one has to offer at your service to make sure that we not only Reduce and Eliminate the Health Problems associated with it but also spread Awareness about the Importance of a Healthy Lifestyle.
