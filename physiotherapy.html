NEURO PHYSICAL THERAPY

It is a specialized branch of Physiotherapy which help to improve the function of patients suffering from physical impairments caused by neurological conditions which are as follow

• Stroke

• Multiple Sclerosis

• Parkinson's Disease

• Vestibular Conditions

• Brain Tuberculosis,

• Traumatic Brain Injury (TBI),

• Facial or Bell's Palsy

• Cerebral Vascular Accidents (CVA) etc.

PEDIATRICS PHYSIOTHERAPY:

Pediatrics Physiotherapy is a field that provides support and treatment service to children. As children are different from adults and they somewhat require different treatment strategies. The treatment for a variety of disorders including:

• Down Syndrome

• Cerebral Palsy(CP)

• Delayed Developmental Milestones,

• Congenital Birth Defects

• Erbs Palsy,

• Foot deformities

• Dystrophies.

SPORTS AND EXERCISE PHYSICAL THERAPY

Sports and Exercise Physiotherapists are involved in the prevention and management of injuries resulting from sport participation at all ages and levels of ability. These specialized physiotherapists provide evidence-based advice on safe participation in sport and exercise. Furthermore, they promote an active lifestyle to aid individuals in improving and maintaining their quality of life. They also play a marvelous role in helping athletes of all ages to promote strengthening abilities and enhance their work performance.

• Sprains and fractures of ankles and knees

• Facial injuries

• Broken or Dislocated Fingers

• Head injuries (concussions and fractures)

• Shoulder fractures or dislocation

• Dislocated knees

• Traumatic head injuries

GERIATRICS PHYSIOTHERAPY

Geriatric Physical therapy is important to provide awareness about the strength and stability of Bones, Muscles, and Joints. It helps to perform daily activities of living in a patient suffering from Alzheimer’s Disease, Parkinson's Disease, and Osteoporosis. We help these patients with the optimized aging process by restoring their motilities and improving their functions.

WOMEN HEALTH PHYSIOTHERAPY

Women Health Physiotherapy is a non-surgical treatment for women. It educates and empower women throughout all ages and stages of their lives. Through young adulthood, pregnancy, postpartum, menopause, pre/post-surgery, after cancer diagnosis/treatment. Womens health physiotherapists treat women with bladder, bowel and sexual problems, and specialize in patients with pelvic floor dysfunction. They also treat patients who are pregnant, with the goal of reducing pain and keeping women fit and active throughout pregnancy. It teaches women how their bodies work properly, how to overcome injury, recover and retrain, how to find their core and inner strength, what strategy they should adopt to being more healthy and fit.

It treats a wide range of women's needs:

• Pregnancy

• Abdominal separation

• Pelvic floor prolapses

• Urinary incontinence

• Constipation

• Antenatal Soft Tissue Release

CHIROPRACTIC

The chiropractor is a health care professional that cares for patients with musculoskeletal system it’s a form of alternative medicine concerned with the diagnosis, treatment and prevention of mechanical disorders of the, especially of the spine. It manages back and neck pain which emphasis on treatment through manual adjustment and manipulation of spine. It focuses on healing, relaxing and providing overall well-being.
