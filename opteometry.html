Doctor of Optometry (OPTOMETRIST)

Doctor of optometry (O.D.s/Optometrists) are essential health care providers. They examine, diagnose, treat and manage diseases and disorders of the eye. And make detailed examination of the health of the eyes.

Optometrist Responsibilities

Optometrist responsibilities include analyzing vision tests, identifying vision problems and prescribing corrective lenses and eyeglasses. Performed detailed orthoptic and low vision assessment, peads refraction and examination, and different diagnosal tests (visual field , color vision , BSV ). They can detect common eye abnormalities and diseases that can lead to permanent vision loss or even blindness. And treat all kinds of patients with kindness and discretion, from young children to the elderly.

Services Provided by Optometry Department of Sehat Medical Complex

• Eye examinations of all age groups.

• Refraction, Prescribing Glasses

• Peads Examination, Cycloplegic Refraction

• Orthoptic Assessment

• Low Vision Assessment

• Retinal Screening for Diabetic Retinopathy and Hypertensive Retinopathy in all types of Diabetic and Hypertensive Patients.

• Examining the eyes of patients of all ages to detect signs of injury, disease, abnormality or vision defects.

• Cataract, Glaucoma Diagnosis and Referral to UOLTH.
