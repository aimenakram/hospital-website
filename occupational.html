Occupational Therapy

Occupational therapy works to improve the life skills or vocational path of our client. It is the form of rehabilitation that helps people overcome or adapt to their functional deficiencies so they can live independently. We treat the physical, mental, developmental; and emotional ailments that impact a patient’s ability to perform day to day task like Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD), Attention Deficit Hyperactive Disorder (ADHD), Down’s Syndrome, Developmental Delays (DD), Cerebral Palsy (CP), Pre Writing Skills, Fine Motor Skills and Activities of Daily Living Skills (ADL’s).
