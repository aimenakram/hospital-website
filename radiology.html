X-RAY IMAGING:

X-rays which are widely used in our medical field is a form of invisible electromagnetic radiation it helps many of doctor professionals to visualize the images of body tissues and making the diagnoses and complexity of diseases more accurately and with authenticity. Here in SEHAT Medical Complex (SMC) we are currently running X-ray Department with latest advancement in technology, well equipped machinery and with standardize precautionary measures to provide you with best of the best results. As to diagnose properly and to treat patient convientenly with proper satisfaction is our top most priority.

X-RAY performing protocol:

> Now let us introduce you with purpose for which X-Ray are mainly performed. These rays use external radiation and while passing through body structures it provide images on specialized plate which are mainly negative and then Cassette Reader (CR) visualize and get further details. The whole body X-ray and Standing Chest X-ray machinery in SMC provides with latest outcome. We have Four sided covered LED walls with provides complete precaution measure to radiologist.

Diagnostic Purpose

x-ray radiography: It involve diagnosing in bone cracks, certain abnormal growths and abnormal masses, pneumonia, calcifications tumors, infections, bone injuries foreign objects and dental issues etc.

Normal Views In X-ray :

Antero-posterior view:

The x-ray beam is exposed from the anterior side of the body part. The beam casts a shadow on the x-ray plate that is kept behind the body part.

Lateral view:

The x-ray beam is exposed from the lateral aspect of the body part.

Oblique view:

In this view the x-ray beam is exposed from a plane which is oblique to the body part on which the x-ray beam is focused.

Special Views:

• Judet view to detect acetabular fracture.

• Merchant view is done for patellofemoral joint.

• Skyline view.

• Dunn view.

• Y view of scapula.
